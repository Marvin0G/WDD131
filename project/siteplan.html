Website Plan: SCD Heritage & Hope

Dynamic Web Fundamentals (WDD 131) Project Planning

Site Name

Name: SCD Heritage & Hope (Nigeria)

Reason for choosing: "Heritage" respects the deep history and evolution of Sickle Cell treatments within Nigeria, while "Hope" emphasizes our focus on home-front crisis management to give families control over the condition.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to act as an educational resource hub for Nigerian families managing Sickle Cell Disease. It will trace historical treatment timelines and provide highly accessible, low-cost crisis mitigation strategies designed to prevent severe pain episodes before they escalate.

Target Audience Scenarios

A typical visitor to this site will likely be a parent, relative, or caregiver looking for quick, trustworthy instructions. Here are two primary questions they might look to answer:

  • "My brother is sweating heavily and has a slight swelling on his forehead; what low-cost, immediate steps can I take right now at home to stop a crisis?"
  • "What is the actual difference in cost and availability between basic daily Folic Acid and newer management therapies like Hydroxyurea in Nigerian teaching hospitals?"

Color Schema

The following colors are selected to keep the site feeling professional, clean, and alert. They are actively demonstrated across this planning document:

Deep Maroon (#800000) - Headers & Important Text
Soft Teal (#1a4d4d) - Nav Bars & Decorative Borders
Warning Gold (#ffcc00) - Highlights & Early Triggers

Typography

  • Headings: Arial Black (or sans-serif fallback) - Used for major section titles to deliver an authoritative, clear layout.
  • Body Text: Verdana (or sans-serif fallback) - Highly readable, wide pixel spacing to ensure stressed caregivers can easily read instructions on any size mobile screen.
